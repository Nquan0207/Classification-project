Training Flow

  • Preprocess image and text inputs separately
  • Encode each modality using dedicated backbones
  • Fuse representations into a shared feature space
  • Train classifier on multimodal embeddings
  • Compare against unimodal baselines
  • Evaluate robustness under incomplete modalities
